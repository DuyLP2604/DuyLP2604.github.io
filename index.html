A tour around Japan

Japan is a country full of breathtaking scenery and extraordinary view. There are a lot of places where tourists can choose to visit. Among them, these are top 3 places that are recommended when traveling in Japan.

Tokyo - The Heart of Modern Japan

Tokyo is the capital of Japan, where many activities in people daily life take place. It is one of the most dynamic cities in the world. Tokyo is famous for:

  • Shibuya street: one of the most busiest and scramblest road intersections in the world
  • Ueno Park: one of the best places in Japan for cherry blossom viewing. Also, it is the home of many traditional arts and a Ueno Zoo, either
  • Akihabara: also known as "Electric Town", famous for anime, manga, video games, books, comic,.. shops
